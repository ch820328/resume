Cross-Platform Business Intelligence Engine

Data Engineering & Productivity
Challenge: Leadership faced a data silo problem: issue tracking was fragmented across Redmine, Excel, and Project Boards, obscuring "Issue Stagnation" and preventing effective bottleneck identification.
Solution: Developed a Laravel-based BI platform with an ETL pipeline to aggregate multi-source data. Designed complex SQL analytics to compute "Unhealthy Rates" (stalled > 7 days) and implemented hierarchical reporting for tiered management visibility.
Impact: Transformed fragmented logs into actionable KPIs. Identified 20%+ stalled tickets previously invisible, leading to a significant reduction in Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) and improved resource allocation efficiency.
