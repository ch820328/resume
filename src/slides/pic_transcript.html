Deterministic BIOS OCR Engine

Computer Vision & Automation
Challenge: Standard ML-based OCR (like Tesseract) only got about 85% accuracy on our low-res BIOS fonts. It didn't understand the layout at all, and for BIOS validation, even a tiny error could break our whole test suite.
Solution: I decided to skip the AI hype and built a deterministic CV engine using OpenCV. I used template matching and custom row-grouping logic to make sure the spatial context stayed perfect every time.
Impact: Pushed our accuracy to over 99% with almost zero latency. This completely freed up the QA team because we could finally automate visual regression testing without worrying about false positives.
