Unified Portal: Visibility into Performance Health

Automation Dashboards & Data Vis
Challenge: Engineers lacked **real-time visibility** into test results across fragmented CLI tools, causing diagnostic delays in identifying **regression trends**.
Solution: Developed a Unified Engineering Portal with WebSocket-driven real-time status monitoring and data visualization dashboards. Aggregated multi-source failure data into a central view.
Impact: Consolidated 10+ tools into a single pane of glass, **reducing setup time by 40%**. Enabled 'One-Click Debugging' and provided clear visibility into performance health for the entire fleet.
